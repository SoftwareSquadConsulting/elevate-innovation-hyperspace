Design. Develop. Deliver.

SINCE 2005, ELEVATE HAS BROUGHT BIG-BRAND IDEAS TO LIFE THROUGH OUR INDUSTRY-LEADING DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT INNOVATIONS.

DESIGN

  • In-house creative team familiar with the retail spirits industry.
  • Quick concept sketches, graphic design, 3d design, technical drawings and CAD services.
  • Fast turnaround times with a precise, efficient and methodical process.
  • Application and software design, virtual & augmented reality, electrical and digital design expertise in house.

DEVELOP

  • 3D Printing for quick prototype concepts.
  • Re-engineering existing products to create a more cost effective and efficient solution.
  • Prototyping in any material including wood, metal, resin, plastic & vacuform extrusions.
  • Small run production capabilities and hand made touches.

We have developed strategic partnerships with experienced factories specifically in the POS business in USA, China and India. Our internal auditing consists of a documented and visual inspection of each factory to make sure they have the tools and machines required to produce the product in a high quality, consistent and timely manner. We have developed and trained a local team that inspects and monitors the production in each phase from the purchasing of the raw materials all the way through manufacturing, packing and final shipping.

DELIVER

  • High quality control standards managed by a full time on site team.
  • Factory direct pricing.
  • Complete logistics solutions from preproduction to drop shipping with less than 1% error rate.

Sedex - Elevate Innovation is a Sedex registered company and we have worked with our partner factories to also get Sedex approved. This year we have instituted the SMETA Best Practice Guide and are requiring a SMETA 2-Pillar audit with all manufacturing partners with the goal to have all facilities audit and pass the 4-Pillar audit by 2017. In addition to second party inspections already performed all facilities understand that independent inspections will also be instituted.
